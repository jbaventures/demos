04 · FAQ

Soft washing, explained

Soft washing is an alternative to pressure washing that doesn't use damaging high pressure. It thoroughly cleans surfaces and eliminates the bacteria that are the root cause of discoloration on roofs and other surfaces.
Soft washing eliminates the microorganisms that cause staining, whereas pressure washing just cleans the surface layer. It also avoids harmful high pressure, and results last far longer.
Roofs, all types of siding, decks, fences, concrete and brick — wood, brick, concrete, aluminum, vinyl, shingles and more.
