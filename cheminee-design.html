The atelier

Cheminée Design Co. develops products and finishes unseen in the industry. Every piece begins as a drawing, becomes a mould in our Boca Raton studio, and ends as the focal point of a room.

Our proprietary stone blends and exotic Scagliola technique — a 17th century Italian art — let us match any marble, any color, any period.
